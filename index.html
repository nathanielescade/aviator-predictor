ABOUT AVIATOR TRADING PREDICTOR

What is this platform?

The Aviator Trading Predictor is an advanced analysis tool designed to help you make informed decisions when playing the Aviator game. It combines real-time market analysis with technical indicators to provide accurate trading signals.

How to use it

  1. Enter your past game multipliers in the input field (minimum 10 rounds recommended)
  2. Click "ANALYZE YOUR PATTERN" to generate trading signals based on your data
  3. Review the trading chart and technical indicators for market trends
  4. Follow the trading signals (BUY, SELL, or HOLD) in the trading panel
  5. Use the "NEXT 5 SIGNALS" to plan your future trades

Understanding the signals

  • BUY - Enter a position when a high multiplier is expected
  • SELL - Exit a position when a low multiplier is expected
  • HOLD - Wait for better trading opportunities

Technical indicators

  • RSI - Relative Strength Index (over 70 = overbought, under 30 = oversold)
  • MA - Moving Average (shows trend direction)
  • MACD - Moving Average Convergence Divergence (momentum indicator)
